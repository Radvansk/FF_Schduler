Fantasy Football Schedule Maker

I created this page because I always used the schedule maker on footballguys.com and they rebuilt their site this year.

Their new schedule maker is a disaster. If your league plays division opponents twice, the new schedule maker no longer sets them for the first three weeks and last three weeks of the season. I think the front and back of the schedule are important for division formats so teams have more potential to control their destiny down the stretch of the regular season.

It will set division games for the first three weeks and then the rest of the division games are completely random. Their page will have teams that play back-to-back in weeks three and four.

This page resolves all of these issues.

Info & Directions

  • This page is designed for 12-team leagues, with 3 divisions, and 14 regular season games.
  • Division opponents play each other twice in weeks 1 - 3 and weeks 12 - 14.
  • Each team will play all non-division teams once.
  • The order you enter your divisions determine the division matchup structure. The first team entered in each division will play the fourth team entered in week 1 and week 12. This is because many leagues I play draft divisions, and its fun that the "drafter" gets to play their first pick in week 1 and their last pick in week 3. Keep this rule in mind when entering the teams in each division.
